Design System

Three circles, one missing corner - a warm, geometric identity. Amber leads, indigo supports, teal accents.

01 - Identity

The mark

Three equal circles in a 2×2 grid with the top-left cell omitted, one per brand color. Keep clear space of at least one circle's width around the mark, and don't recolor the circles outside the palette.
